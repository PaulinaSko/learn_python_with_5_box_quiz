Few words about 5_box_quiz - how it`s working?


The goal is to learn Python through repeating 100 flashcards. How?

If You see the question think carefully. If You know the answear click "I know" button and check the answear. If You are not so sure about the correct answear choose "Next question" button so the flashcard will wait for another try. Repeat these steps until You know all the answears. Then the certificate will appear. Then You can say "I really know this. I'm really good at it"
And You are right, You are just P-H-E-N-O-M-E-N-A-L :-)


P.S. Questions are mainly from Python, but not only. Good luck!
